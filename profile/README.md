## 👋 Welcome to the CloudRader community!

[CloudRader](https://cloudrader.com) is an open-source initiative focused on building modular, self-hostable apps for organizations and communities.

Our goal is to create a connected platform of independent services — from reservation systems and access management to inventory and internal infrastructure tools — designed to work well both separately and together.

CloudRader is still in an early stage, but the vision is clear:
build practical open-source software with a cloud-native mindset, strong interoperability, and transparent development.

### 🚀 Projects

- **Reservium** — a reservation and resource booking system
- **Inventarium** *(planned)* — asset and equipment management

### 🌈 Philosophy

- Open-source and transparent
- Self-hostable by default
- Modular microservice-oriented architecture
- Designed for extensibility and integration
- Built for real organizational needs

### ☁️ Why “CloudRader”?

CloudRader is inspired by the idea of riding into the world of cloud technologies and conquering its complexity step by step.

We build open-source tools, experiment with distributed systems, and try to make infrastructure less painful for organizations and developers alike.

Sometimes the cloud conquers us back — but that's just part of the deployment process.
